What to do next

If the result suggests pneumonia

Intelli-Monia is not a diagnosis. Use this as a conversation starter with a professional.

Next steps:
  • Consult a clinician for a formal read of the X-ray and a physical exam.
  • Share symptoms: fever, cough, shortness of breath, chest pain, fatigue.
  • Seek urgent care if breathing is difficult or symptoms are severe.

If the result suggests normal

A ‚Äúnormal‚Äù result does not guarantee everything is fine. Symptoms matter.

Next steps:
  • If symptoms persist, consult a clinician anyway.
  • Consider image quality: poor lighting or low resolution can reduce accuracy.
  • Retest with a clearer image if available and compare confidence.
